But why would I take this nickname? Haha, who knows. Sometimes, I will register myself at websites, like Microsoft, with the family name of "Song" and the given name of "Huadan". Then, to the latter part "88", one may think that its meaning is "eighty-eight", but it is not. In fact, it means "father", because in Chinese, "father" sounds like "88". So, this is all I want to say, and my nickname "songhuadan88" means "the Father of Century Egg".

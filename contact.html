I would like to add some sentences on my nickname "songhuadan88". It has been a long time since I took the nickname and I do not know exactly how long, either, but a lot of people, even some of my best friends, do not know the exact meaning of my nickname. The former part "songhuadan" is the pinyin form of "松花蛋", which is a Chinese word and means a kind of food, Century Egg. Actually, I do not like this food and seldom eat it. But why would I take this nickname? Haha, who knows. Sometimes, I will register myself at websites, like Microsoft, with the family name of "Song" and the given name of "Huadan". It is not difficult to find that this is a separation of my nickname, but since "松花蛋" is a complete word in Chinese, this is somewhat wrong. Then, to the latter part "88", one may think that its meaning is "eighty-eight", but it is not. In fact, it means "father", because in Chinese, "father" sounds like "88". So, this is all I want to say, and my nickname "songhuadan88" means "the Father of Century Egg".
